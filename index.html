Needledust

Hand Crafted by some of India's finest artisans,
NEEDLEDUST brings to you a line of handcrafted fine leather juttis
that speak the charm of a true old school artisan with the design sensibilities of a modern,
luxury shoe brand. Therefore, re creating the jutti! With love for the handicraft and passion
for revival of the jutti, Shirin Mann Sangha launched NEEDLEDUST in May 2014, amalgamating the
finesse of old royal moulds with innovative design and embroidery patterns.

Pastel and Pop

Inspired by the vibrant heritage of India, Pastels and Pop brings to you a refreshing perspective
on authentic Indian handicraft through an assortment of beautifully crafted juttis,
which have redefined the industry standard for grace and grandeur.
The Chhabra sisters, Akanksha, and Aarti, started Pastels and Pop back in July 2015 to introduce
this beautiful punjabi handicraft to the people in South India.
